Royale Pro League, what are we?

A Clash Royale league focused on filling your needs

We are the Royale Pro League, a new league non-affiliated with Supercell. We do our best to provide to the community great events, great prizes and great time!

The main event we're running is the Royale Pro League, consisted of the best Clash Royale players, but that's not it. We're also hosting tournaments on a weekly basis for the casual gamer in you or the competitive Clasher you are! We have partnered with Coca-Cola to provide the community the best we can achieve, but we're still highering our standarts and coming up with new ways to entertain, but also reward the community, players and viewers.

The first season of the Royale Pro League will give the opportunity to a maximum of 1024 players to prove they can be the Clash Royale best player in the world. Out of the 1024, only 64 will battle for the top pool place, for the higher seed. Then, the top 32 will be invited to the biggest tournament ever seen in the history of Clash Royale, with a whooping prize pool of 10,000$ USD! Only the best player shall be worthy of this prize!
